Optimize Multi-Company Communication

Dynamic email address and signature change across companies


  • Creates company-specific user emails and signatures in a multi-company environment.

  • Changes the user's email and signature to the ones configured in the company, or keeps the previous ones if none is configured.

  • When switching companies, your email address and signature will change according to the ones defined in the current company.
